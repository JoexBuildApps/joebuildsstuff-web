Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

betterDriver is developed by JoeBuildsStuff. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use the betterDriver mobile application.

Information We Collect

betterDriver does not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information with third parties. All data generated by the app is stored locally on your device.

Location Data

betterDriver uses your device's GPS to calculate your speed in real time. This location data is used exclusively within the app to display your current speed and track trip statistics. Location data is never transmitted to external servers.

Trip Data

Trip history, scores, and driving statistics are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to JoeBuildsStuff or any third party.

Device Information

When you contact us for support, the app may include your device model and Android version in the email body to help us assist you. This information is only shared if you choose to send a support email.

Third-Party Services

betterDriver does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not contain ads.

Children's Privacy

betterDriver is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at soporte@joebuildsstuff.net
